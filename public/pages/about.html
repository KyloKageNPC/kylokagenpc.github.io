About

I'm a product designer obsessed with building exceptional digital experiences that bridge the gap between user needs and business goals.

My Journey

I started my design career at a digital agency where I worked on projects for Fortune 500 companies. After gaining experience across diverse industries, I became fascinated by the intersection of design thinking and product strategy.
