CASE STUDY

Fintech App Redesign

Redesigning a mobile banking application to improve user experience, increase engagement, and reduce transaction friction for over 100,000 daily users.

MOBILE APP FINTECH 2024

ROLE

Lead Product Designer

DURATION

6 Months

TEAM

5 Designers, 10 Engineers

PLATFORM

iOS & Android

The Challenge

The existing fintech application had a confusing navigation structure that led to high abandonment rates during transaction flows. Users reported difficulty completing payments, and the app's performance was inconsistent across devices.

Our team was tasked with redesigning the core experience to reduce friction, improve clarity, and establish a modern design system that could scale across future features.

Key Metrics

  • Transaction Success Rate +38%
  • Avg. Session Duration +42%
  • User Retention (30-day) +25%
  • App Rating 4.7★

Design Process

01

Research & Discovery

Conducted user interviews, analyzed usage patterns, and identified pain points in transaction flows.

02

Wireframing & Testing

Created low-fidelity prototypes and conducted usability testing with 50+ users across different segments.

03

Visual Design & Handoff

Implemented high-fidelity designs, created design system documentation, and collaborated with engineering.

Results

The redesigned app resulted in a 38% increase in successful transactions and significantly improved user satisfaction scores. The new design system enabled faster feature development and maintained consistency across the platform.

Post-launch analytics show sustained improvements in user engagement and retention, with the app now consistently rated 4.7★ on both iOS and Android app stores.
