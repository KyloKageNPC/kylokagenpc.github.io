CASE STUDY

Enterprise SaaS Platform

Building a comprehensive web platform for enterprise clients managing complex workflows and data analytics at scale.

WEB PLATFORM ENTERPRISE 2024

ROLE

Lead Product Designer, Design Lead

DURATION

8 Months

TEAM

8 Designers, 15 Engineers

IMPACT

$2.5M revenue increase

The Challenge

The enterprise SaaS space demanded a platform that could handle complex data workflows while maintaining intuitive navigation. Clients required real-time collaboration features, advanced reporting, and customizable workflows.

We designed a modular system that provides flexibility and power without overwhelming users, utilizing progressive disclosure and contextual help throughout the interface.

Key Metrics

  • User Adoption Rate +92%
  • Support Tickets (Reduction) -67%
  • Time to First Value 3 days
  • NPS Score 72

Design Approach

01

Enterprise Discovery

Extensive stakeholder interviews with 20+ enterprise clients to understand pain points and workflows.

02

Design System

Built comprehensive design system with 100+ components to ensure consistency across 40+ screens.

03

Implementation

Partnered with engineering to implement responsive design that works seamlessly across all devices.

Outcomes

The platform launched with immediate enterprise adoption, generating $2.5M in new revenue within the first quarter. Companies using the platform reported 45% improvement in team productivity.

The design system became the foundation for future product development, reducing time to market for new features by 60%.
